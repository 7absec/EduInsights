The various Railway/Bus Passes for student issued by State Transport Service

Railway concession for"Girls":

  • Indian Railways offers free-of-cost travel facility to school- and college-going girls on MST (monthly season ticket) of general class. This concession can be availed by girls up to graduation.
Apart from the above scheme passes are also been issued for Monthly/Quarterly/Half yearly and Yearly basis for local train services which are detailed below
  • Rs. 00.00 for one month.
  • Rs. 200.00 for three months.
  • Rs. 400.00 for six months.
  • Rs. 800.00 for one year.
    • Documents Required
    • 1 No. Passport Size photograph.
    • Student Id & Proof of address.
    • An application in plain paper.

Railway concession for"Boys":

  • Boys can avail free travel on general class MST in Indian Railways up to Class XII. The facility of free-of-cost travel in the trains on general class MST is also available to the students of registered madrasas.
Apart from the above scheme passes are also been issued for Monthly/Quarterly/Half yearly and Yearly basis for local train services which are detailed below
  • Rs. 100.00 for one month.
  • Rs. 300.00 for three months.
  • Rs. 600.00 for six months.
  • Rs. 1200.00 for one year.
    • Documents Required
    • 1 No. Passport Size photograph.
    • Student Id & Proof of address.
    • An application in plain paper.

BUS Concession for"Girls":

  • Girls of various Government/Private educational institutions (which are Govt. recognized/Govt. aided/Govt. affiliated) including Polytechnic and ITI etc. can avail the facility of this bus pass for Ordinary/Express buses. A fifty percent (40%) of concession shall be given on the prevailing fare in Express bus services (fractions to be rounded off to next rupee).
Apart from the above scheme passes are also been issued for Monthly/Quarterly/Half yearly and Yearly basis for Ordinary bus services which are detailed below
  • Rs. 200.00 for one month.
  • Rs. 600.00 for three months.
  • Rs. 1200.00 for six months.
  • Rs. 2300.00 for one year.
Documents Required
  • 1 No. Passport Size photograph.
  • Attested copy of Identity Card issued by Institution.
  • An application in plain paper.

BUS Concession for"BOYS":

  • Boys of various Government/Private educational institutions (which are Govt. recognized/Govt. aided/Govt. affiliated) including Polytechnic and ITI etc. can avail the facility of this bus pass for Ordinary/Express buses. A fifty percent (50%) of concession shall be given on the prevailing fare in Express bus services (fractions to be rounded off to next rupee).
Apart from the above scheme passes are also been issued for Monthly/Quarterly/Half yearly and Yearly basis for Ordinary bus services which are detailed below
  • Rs. 250.00 for one month.
  • Rs. 700.00 for three months.
  • Rs. 1300.00 for six months.
  • Rs. 2400.00 for one year.
Documents Required
  • 1 No. Passport Size photograph.
  • Attested copy of Identity Card issued by Institution.
  • An application in plain paper.

Railway/Bus concession for Physically Handicapped Students:

  • A physically handicapped student with 40% disability and above can avail the facility of free bus pass and no fare shall be charged in Ordinary/Express bus services. This Pass is issued for a period until there academic complition.
Documents Required
  • 1 No. Passport Size photograph.
  • Attested copy of Disability Certificate issued by Directorate of Social Welfare.
  • Attested copy of Medical Certificate issued by Directorate of Health Services.
  • An application in plain paper.
